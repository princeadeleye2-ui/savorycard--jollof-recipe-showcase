Classic Nigerian Jollof Rice

A smoky, flavorful West African rice dish perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long grain rice
  • 3 fresh tomatoes
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups chicken stock

Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and onion.
  2. Fry tomato paste in oil for 3 minutes.
  3. Add blended mixture and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Add seasoning, thyme, curry, and salt.
  5. Pour in chicken stock and bring to boil.
  6. Add washed rice, stir, and cover.
  7. Cook on low heat for 20–25 minutes.
  8. Fluff and serve hot.
